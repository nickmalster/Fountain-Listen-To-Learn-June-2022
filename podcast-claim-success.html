Podcaster Wallet

Earning Bitcoin made easy for every podcaster

Getting your show on the Lightning Network and seeing all the data should be easy. However, no simple user-friendly solutions exist for podcasters today. That's why we're launching the Podcaster Wallet. etc.
Join the Lightning Network in the Fountain app
See your show's earnings and supporters
Read and reply to messages from all apps
Split your earnings with other users

Success!

You can now start receiving payments from listeners on Fountain and other podcast apps that support Value for Value. Close and reopen the Fountain app and go to your Wallet to see a summary card for your show. Click it to go to your show page where you will see a detailed view of all payments and messages from your supporters. To learn how to get started with Value for Value, check out The Fountain Guide to Value for Value. Send us an email us at hello@fountain.fm if you need further support. Thank you.
