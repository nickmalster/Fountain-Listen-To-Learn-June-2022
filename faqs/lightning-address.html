What is a Lightning Address?

Every Fountain user has a Lightning Address connected to their Wallet. A Lightning Address is like an email address that you can use to receive Bitcoin over the Lightning Network. To receive money, share your Lightning Address and you can get paid from any Bitcoin Lightning Wallet. You can share your Lightning Address from your Wallet and your Profile.
