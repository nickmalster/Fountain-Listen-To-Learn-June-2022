How do I earn on Fountain?

On Fountain you earn small units of Bitcoin called sats. There are 100 million sats in one Bitcoin. When you earn sats they will be added to your wallet balance. In your Wallet you can also see how many sats you have earned in a given period and find a history of your recent transactions. Why am I getting paid? Every minute that you spend consuming content, creating content, or watching ads, increases the value of the platform you’re using. Think about the ones you use every day. Youtube, Twitter, Facebook, Instagram, TikTok - all of these platforms would lose their value without the time and attention that you give them, but none of them reward you for it. Fountain is different. Fountain is the only podcast app where both listeners and podcasters get rewarded for the value they bring to the platform. How do I earn on Fountain? Listen to podcasts Get paid by Fountain for your first hour of daily podcast listening. The earning rate changes every day and it’s completely random. The earnings meter at the top of the player will tell you how much you have earned. Listen to promotions Get paid by podcasters and advertisers for listening to their promoted content. The earning rate is set by the promoter. You will see one promoted episode and one promoted clip on the Home page every day. Clips and comments Get paid by other listeners when they like your clips or comments. The default amount for likes can be configured in the settings and you can press the like button as many times as you want. You can also earn by referring friends to Fountain. You can learn more about referrals here. How much will I earn from listening to podcasts? Click on the earnings meter at the top of the player to check your earning rate today. Your rate is assigned at random every day. The rate is affected by your playback speed. For example, if your daily rate is 10 sats per minute, you will earn 10 sats if you are listening on 1x speed. If you increase your playback speed to 2x, you will earn 5 sats per minute. If your earning rate is less than 1 sat per minute, you'll only have a chance of earning 1 sat. For example, if your daily rate is 1 sat per minute but you increase your playback speed to 2x, your rate will be 0.5 sats per minute. This means you will have a 50% chance of earning 1 sat every minute you listen. If you increase your playback speed to 3x, your rate would be 0.3 sats per minute and you will have a 30% chance of earning 1 sat every minute. Finally, if you skip forward or back while you listen, or if your device loses connection, your earnings will be affected and you may not earn the full amount. Why am I not earning? If your are not earning, you will see a grey lightning bolt at the top of the player. Press it for more information. You've reached the daily limit Fountain only pays you for your first hour of daily podcast listening. You can still earn more by listening to promotions, or you can come back tomorrow to continue earning. Your earnings are paused If our security system notices something irregular about your account, your earnings will be temporarily paused. If you're playing fair this shouldn't happen, but sometimes our system can throw up false positives. You won't be able to earn if your device doesn't have mobile data or an active WiFi connection. In this instance, the earnings counter will simply not tick up.
