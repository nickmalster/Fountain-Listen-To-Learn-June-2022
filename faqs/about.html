What is Fountain?

Incentives on the internet are broken. Fountain fixes this with programmable money. Whether you’re a content creator, consumer or curator, on Fountain you get rewarded for the value you provide for others. Unlike the attention economy, Fountain uses money as a signal to surface the content that is truly valuable. Fountain is powered by the Bitcoin Lightning Network and is available on iOS and Android.
